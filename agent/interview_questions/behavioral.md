# Behavioral Interview Questions

Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) when evaluating responses.

## Teamwork & Collaboration

1. **Tell me about a time you had a disagreement with a teammate about a technical decision. How did you resolve it?**
   - Look for: communication skills, empathy, willingness to compromise, focus on outcomes over ego.

2. **Describe a situation where you had to work with someone whose working style was very different from yours.**
   - Look for: adaptability, patience, ability to find common ground.

3. **Tell me about a time you helped a colleague who was struggling with a task.**
   - Look for: mentorship ability, generosity with time, teaching skills.

## Problem-Solving Under Pressure

4. **Describe a production incident you were involved in. What happened and what did you do?**
   - Look for: calm under pressure, systematic debugging, communication during crisis, post-mortem thinking.

5. **Tell me about a time you had to deliver a project with an extremely tight deadline. How did you manage it?**
   - Look for: prioritization, scope management, communication with stakeholders, quality tradeoffs.

## Growth & Learning

6. **Tell me about a time you made a significant mistake at work. What happened and what did you learn?**
   - Look for: ownership, humility, concrete lessons learned, process improvements.

7. **Describe a situation where you had to learn a new technology or framework quickly to complete a project.**
   - Look for: learning strategies, resourcefulness, ability to become productive quickly.

## Leadership & Initiative

8. **Tell me about a time you identified a problem that nobody else noticed and took the initiative to fix it.**
   - Look for: proactiveness, ownership mentality, ability to see the bigger picture.

9. **Describe a situation where you had to convince others to adopt a new approach or technology.**
   - Look for: influence skills, data-driven arguments, ability to handle resistance.

10. **Tell me about a project you're most proud of. What made it special?**
    - Look for: passion, impact awareness, ability to articulate value, technical depth.
